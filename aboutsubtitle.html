    Antara - CEO of the ArabianCamels.io

    Antara is an Irish serial entrepreneur and storm chaser, and has been involved in various ventures across different industries. As an avid martial artist, in his early years he travelled the world looking for surreal experiences, including to the mountain peaks of Wudang, China, to study martial arts. He worked for years in the City if London in Equities, Wealth Management and Real Estate before moving into the world of transmedia and film production. He is the writer, creator and producer of the Antara film franchise and IP, and is the creator of Movie NFTs, a ground-breaking concept that gives NFT holders access to the exciting world of movie production. Antara lives with his family in London, loves horses, and holds a BSC Degree in Politics and is qualified in financial regulation, risk and taxation and securities from London’s Chartered Institute of Securities and investments.
